Eligibility Criteria for MBBS in Belarus.
There are some eligibility criteria that must be met for MBBS in Belarus
  • Belarus has free merit system for international students
  • You must be at least 17 years of age on or before 31st December of the admission year
  • You must have completed 12th standard with science degree
  • Your subjects must include Chemistry and Biology
  • You must secure at least 55% in your 12th standard exam
  • NEET has become mandatory now after the new rules by NMC
  • The admission process is quite simple and convenient for all international students
Required Documents for MBBS Admission in Belarus 2022-2023
  • Scanned mark sheet copies of 10th and 12th standard
  • Birth certificate
  • A valid passport
  • Migration certificate
  • Bank statement of your guardian to confirm that you can afford the course fee and living expense
  • Medical certificate that has the complete reports on HIV and vaccination
  • Passport size photos
  • Application form that must be filled up
  • Offer letter from the Belarus mbbs university
  • Air tickets
  • Scanned copies of NEET scorecard
  • These are the documents needed and you must show them in time of admission
  • The documents must be 100% original
  • If there is any issue with any of the document there might be issues with admission
Step by Step Admission Procedure for MBBS/MD in Belarus
The admission process for MBBS in Belarus is quite simple and convenient for all MBBS aspirants.
  • As mentioned before, Belarus offers free merit system for all international students
  • The applicants scan their secondary school certificate with copy of travel passport
  • It is done for eligibility assessment and this takes 1-2 days
  • After that the applicants transfers the MBBS course fee
  • The application form with documents are forwarded for processing and approval at the department of migration
  • The processing might take up to 2 weeks
  • Then the offer letter is sent to the applicant
  • Once they receive the offer letter they can continue their VISA process
  • Once you receive the student VISA, you have to book a flight to Belarus
  • Get admitted in MBBS university in Belarus and fulfil your dream
  • The documents must be 100% original and legal
  • Do not make any mistakes while filling up the application form
  • Any kind of wrong information can lead to the cancellation of your admission
  • For any queries, you can reach out to the consultancy firm or the university support system
