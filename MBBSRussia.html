MBBS in Russia

Russia is one of the best places to pursue a carrier in MBBS. It is the most preferred destination for all aspiring medical students. MBBS universities in RUSSIA provides the best facilities for all the medical students and also allows who hasn’t passed the test of 2 languages (IELTS + TOEFL). The country is very much liberating in these cases. MBBS IN RUSSIA is a platform where medical students can ensure themselves to get a high-paying job in some top medical farms and hospitals throughout the whole world. Students who want to study medicine in RUSSIA and also have 50% marks can apply for the Scholarship. Among 35 European Medical universities, Russia provides facilities over almost 15 universities, which are recommended by 3 leading medical council, namely (WHO + UNESCO & NMC). "MBBS IN RUSSIA" for Indian students from CBSE and ICSE boards is also available. The duration of the course will be 5 years, and in addition to that, Medical Education in RUSSIA also gives an opportunity for 1 year of internship. MBBS fees in Russia for this duration will be very much affordable and cheaper than other international universities. Medical Admission in Russia will open up an ocean of opportunities for aspiring medical students and help them to build a bright future

Duration of MBBS Course in Russia
  • The total duration of MBBS course in Russia is "6 years" for the students who have chosen English as their medium of studies.
  • The duration is seven years for those who have chosen Russian as their medium.
  • Although, students who have chosen English as their medium are also taught basic Russian.

Five valuable years of your academics in MBBS abroad Ukraine gives you the right amount of exposure.

Medium of Teaching in Russia.
  • There are some medical universities in Russia that offers MBBS courses in Russia
  • Then there are other medical universities who offer "MBBS courses in English medium" for the international students
  • Although, English medium students are also taught a few basic elementary level Russian
Eligibility for MBBS in Russia
  • You should be at least 17 and maximum 25 years old on or before 31st December of the admission year.
  • You have passed Class 12th with Science degree, with PCM and English subjects from a board recognized by the Indian authorities.
  • You have secured at least 50% in your 10+2.
  • You are SC/OBC/ST and have secured 45% in 12th Standard; subject remains the same.
  • You have qualified NEET Exam.
Documents Requirement for MBBS Admission in Russia 2021-2022
  • The Passport which has a minimum of 18 months of validity.
  • 10th Certificate and Mark Sheet
  • 12th Certificate and Mark Sheet
  • Birth Certificate in English or translated into, notarized.
  • The Photo – 10 (4.5 x 3.5 cm, 70% face)
  • An official invitation letter from the Russian University
  • HIV test documents
  • Authorized documents from the Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi
  • Legalization of all the documents from the Russian Embassy
  • Visa fees
  • Bank receipt of University 1st year Tuition Fees Payment (It is required by some universities)
